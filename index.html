About Dr. Smangele Shandu

PhD in Criminology and Forensic Studies

Dr. Smangele Shandu is a dedicated professional with expertise in Social Work, Criminology, and Academia. As a Private Social Worker, Dr. Shandu provides compassionate therapy and crisis intervention, assisting individuals and families in overcoming challenges related to mental health, addiction, and trauma.

As a Criminologist, Dr. Shandu analyzes the root causes of crime and advocates for effective criminal justice reform. In Academia, Dr. Shandu teaches, conducts research, and inspires students to explore the intersections of Social Work, Criminology, and Social Justice.

Blending practical experience with scholarly insights, Dr. Shandu's work promotes rehabilitation, social change, and holistic support for those affected by crime and social inequality.
